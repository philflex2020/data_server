Telegraf silent-drop demo

Telegraf holds metrics in a RAM buffer before flushing to InfluxDB. When the buffer fills it silently discards the oldest metrics — no error, no alert. Parquet captures everything.

  1. In the Telegraf → InfluxDB2 box, set Limit → 1 k and click Apply & Restart
  2. Wait ~10 s for telegraf to come back up (Buffer shows 0 / 1000)
  3. Click 0.1 s rate button — rate jumps to ~11,500 msg/s
  4. Watch the Buffer fill bar go red and Dropped count climb
  5. Run a count comparison (route = Both, queries running) — InfluxDB row count will be lower than DuckDB
  6. Click 1 s to restore normal rate, set Limit → 100 k to recover

At 1 s / 1,152 cells the 5 k buffer fills in ~4 s. At 0.1 s it fills in under 1 s. InfluxDB never knows — telegraf logs nothing by default.
